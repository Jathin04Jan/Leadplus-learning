LeadPlus Intent Search

Job postings are buying signals. Search them, get ranked companies with the evidence.

Lab environment — not production. The corpus is a synthetic replica (386 job postings across 301 companies), so results demonstrate that the architecture works; they say nothing about real-world relevance. There is no auth, no multi-tenancy and no data-pack gating — every company in the pool is visible to you. A real tenant would see a gated subset.

Ask a question to begin.
The query is parsed by an LLM into chips, and nothing after that is an LLM: retrieval is lexical ts_rank + exact cosine, fused with reciprocal rank fusion, then scored by a fixed formula. Identical chips always return an identical ranking.
Positive terms never filter — they score. A company matching 1 of 3 groups is ranked lower, never dropped. Hard chips (locations, segments, NAICS/SIC, LinkedIn, sizes and exclusions) do remove companies — that is what makes them facts rather than guesses, and every exclusion is listed back to you with what it deleted.
A question this app cannot answer gets refused, not answered badly.
